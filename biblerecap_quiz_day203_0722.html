Question 1 of 10 Detail

DETAIL — Isaiah 28:1-4 pictures Ephraim's "fading flower" of pride giving way to Assyria's storm. What image does verse 4 use for how quickly that fading glory will be consumed?

Question 2 of 10

Isaiah 28:9-13 records the priests and prophets mocking Isaiah's preaching with the phrase "line upon line, line upon line; precept upon precept, precept upon precept; here a little, there a little." What does this mockery reveal about their attitude toward his message?

Question 3 of 10

Isaiah 28:16 announces a "tried stone, a precious cornerstone, a sure foundation" laid in Zion — a verse later applied to Christ in the New Testament. What does the verse promise to the one who trusts in it?

Question 4 of 10 Detail

DETAIL — Isaiah 28's closing farming parable contrasts how different crops are threshed. According to verses 27-28, which crop is beaten out with a stick rather than crushed by a cart wheel or threshing sledge?

Question 5 of 10

Isaiah 29:1 opens a woe oracle against "Ariel." What city does the verse identify Ariel as, calling it "the city where David encamped"?

Question 6 of 10 Detail

DETAIL — Isaiah 29:13 diagnoses a worship that looks devout but isn't — a verse Jesus later quotes against the Pharisees. How does the verse describe the problem?

Question 7 of 10

Isaiah 29:16 rebukes the people's "turning of things upside down" by comparing it to an absurd reversal. What comparison does the verse use?

Question 8 of 10

Isaiah 30 condemns Judah's rebellious envoys for making a costly journey through the Negev, loading treasure onto donkeys and camels to secure protection. Which nation were they seeking an alliance with instead of trusting the LORD?

Question 9 of 10 Detail

DETAIL — Isaiah 30:15 states plainly what would have brought Judah salvation and strength, and just as plainly that they refused it. What does the verse say?

Question 10 of 10

Isaiah 30:26 pictures the day the LORD binds up his people's brokenness with an image of intensified light. What does the verse say will happen to the sun?
